The Foundational Claim: R = O . M . P

Observation (O)

The Act of Attention.

The capacity to receive and register input. In the model, its rate of change is driven by an external bias (the Global Prior) and internal conflict.

Memory (M)

The Repository of Experience.

The accumulated history that contextualizes new input. It stabilizes the system and modulates the response to novel stimuli (O).

Pattern (P)

The Coherence Principle.

The organizational force that structures information. It allows the system to predict and to stabilize the energetic cost of awareness.

Reality (R) is the emergent coherence arising from the multiplicative interaction of O, M, and P.
