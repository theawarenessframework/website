Return and Refund Policy

Last updated: October 30, 2025

1. Eligibility and Return Window

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase of The Awareness Framework. You must initiate a return request within 7 calendar days of receiving your physical book.

To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. This means the book must have no visible signs of wear, creasing, writing, or damage. It must also be in the original packaging, if applicable.

2. Damaged or Defective Books

If you receive a book that is damaged due to shipping or contains a manufacturing defect (e.g., missing pages, inverted print), please contact us immediately.

  • You must report the damage within 7 days of delivery.
  • Please provide your Order Number and clear photographs of the damage.
  • We will cover all costs associated with returning the damaged item and shipping you a replacement.

3. How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, please send an email to our support team with the subject line: "Return Request - [Your Order Number]" or if purchased through Whatsapp, contact via Whatsapp.

Email Address: mail@sanjeevkhanal.com.np

In your email, please include: (a) Your Order Number, (b) The reason for the return, and (c) Photos if the item is damaged. We will respond with return shipping instructions within 2-3 business days.

4. Refunds and Shipping Costs

  • Return Shipping: You will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item unless the return is due to damage or an error on our part. Shipping costs are non-refundable.
  • Processing: Once we receive your returned book and confirm its condition, we will process your refund.
  • Refund Method: A credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment (e.g., online payment, cash on delivery (COD), credit card, PayPal) within 7-10 business days.

Please note that this policy applies only to physical book purchases. All digital products (e-books, audiobooks, etc.), if offered, are non-refundable once downloaded.
